Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the %%SERVER_NAME%% verification service (the “Service”) collects, how it is used, and how you can delete it. We follow a principle of data minimization: we store only what is needed to link your accounts and nothing more.

1. Information we store

  • Discord user ID — the numeric ID of your Discord account.
  • Roblox user ID and username — your numeric Roblox ID and current username.
  • Server (guild) ID — which server you verified in.
  • Timestamps — when you first verified and when your link was last updated, and (if applicable) the staff member who force-verified you.

Temporarily, while you are verifying, we also process a single-use signed link identifier (valid 10 minutes) and a short-lived OAuth “state” value. These are not long-term records.

2. Information we do not store

  • We never receive or store your Discord or Roblox password.
  • We do not store Roblox OAuth access or refresh tokens. The access token is used once to read your public Roblox profile and is then discarded.
  • We do not collect your email address, payment information, IP-based profile, or any Roblox data beyond your public profile.

3. How we use your information

  • To confirm you control the Roblox account and link it to your Discord account.
  • To assign and manage your verified role in Discord.
  • To enable in-game (ER:LC) roleplay features that rely on the Discord ↔ Roblox link.
  • To prevent one Roblox account from being linked to multiple Discord accounts.

4. Roblox authorization scope

When you authorize with Roblox we request only the openid profile scope, which provides your public profile (user ID, username, display name, avatar). We do not request access to messages, friends, inventory, or the ability to act on your behalf.

5. Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. Your data is stored on the self-hosted %%SERVER_NAME%% server and is accessible only to the bot and authorized staff. It is shared with Discord and Roblox only to the extent inherent in using their platforms (e.g., assigning a role).

6. Retention & deletion

We keep your link until you remove it. You can delete your stored data at any time:

  • Run /unlink in Discord — this immediately deletes your link record and removes your verified role.
  • Expired verification links and OAuth state values are automatically purged.
  • You may also ask staff to delete your data; we will honor verified requests.

7. Security

Verification links and OAuth state are cryptographically signed and time-limited. Links are single-use. We recommend the Service is served over HTTPS. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

8. Children

The Service is intended for users who meet Discord’s and Roblox’s minimum age requirements. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone below those ages.

9. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be announced in the %%SERVER_NAME%% Discord server, and the “Last updated” date above will change.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact the %%SERVER_NAME%% staff team via our Discord server.
